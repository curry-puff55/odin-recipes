Chirashi

Home

Description

Chirashi is a rice bowl topped with slices of sashimi-grade raw salmon, cucumber, and avocado.

    Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine
  • 75 g avocado
  • 125 g cucumber
  • 200g sashimi-grade salmon

    Method

  1. Pour soy sauce and rice wine into a small pan and boil it over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Cut salmon, avocado and cucumber into cubes.
  3. Pour the soy sauce marinade over the avocado and salmon, and leave it to marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve over cooked rice.
