Description

Yukinabe is a one-pot dish that is easy on your stomach.

Minced pork is simmered with grated daikon and ginger to produce a mild and warming dish.

    Ingredients

  • 200g minced pork
  • 200g radish
  • 20g ginger
  • 1 tbsp rice wine
  • 1 tbsp dashi mix
  • Spring onion leaves

    Method

  1. Grate the daikon radish and ginger.
  2. Place the daikon radish and ginger in a pot.
  3. Arrange the minced pork on the grated radish and ginger mixture.
  4. Pour the rice wine and dashi mix over the ingredients.
  5. Boil the pot on medium heat and cover. When it begins to boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer.
  6. Garnish with a general amount of spring onion leaves.
