Dakgalbi

Home

Description

Dakgalbi is a Korean inspired chicken stir fry.

Chopped chicken is marinated in a spicy sauce mix then stir fried with sweet potatoes, green cabbage, and carrots.

    Ingredients

  • 200g chicken
  • 1/2 medium sweet potato
  • 100g cabbage
  • 100g carrots
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp rice wine
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger

    Method

  1. Combine the gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, rice wine, garlic and ginger in a bowl and mix them well.
  2. Pour the sauce over the chopped chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Arrange the vegetables in a skillet and add the marinated meal on top.
  4. Cook on medium high heat for 3-4 minutes, then reduce the heat to medium and cook until chicken is no longer pink.
  5. Serve with rice.
